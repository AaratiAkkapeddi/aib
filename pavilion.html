Instead, designers work with the AI to interpret each design into a series of uniform joints and beams that will be easy to fabricate and assemble.

While the beams for this design can be cut from any available lumber, each of the metal joints must be custom made. No two of them are alike. Each joint is responsible for positioning multiple beams at specific angles to ensure everything fits together. media description: video shows the joint pieces being cast and installed. A 3d animation shows each joint piece laid out next to each other in a column.

A total of 60 pieces of 2x6 lumber are used to create the entire pavilion. This same amount of material would only create a small shed using traditional construction techniques.
